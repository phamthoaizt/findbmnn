font manual

1.
access opentype features in adobe illustrator and adobe indesign

go to window > type > opentype  (alt+shift+ctrl+t). in the opentype panel,
simply click on the button to activate the opentype feature that you like.

2.
access all alternate characters in adobe illustrator and adobe indesign

go to window > type > glyphs . the default on the ‘show’ dropdown list bar
should be ‘entire font’, you can change it to every features that you like. you
can also select a character on the font and then change the show list to
‘alternates for current selection’ to access alternate characters that available for
that character.
